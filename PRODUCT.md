# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Spanish freelancers and autónomos — self-employed professionals navigating quarterly tax obligations (modelo 303, 130, etc.). They are capable adults who feel overwhelmed by tax bureaucracy, not by lack of intelligence. They use Klaro on a laptop or phone, typically under mild stress, looking for relief and clarity. The job to be done: file correctly and on time without needing a gestoría.

## Product Purpose

Klaro makes taxes legible for Spanish freelancers. It removes friction from the quarterly filing cycle, turning a dreaded obligation into a routine task. Success looks like: visitor lands → understands the value in 10 seconds → signs up → files their first tax return without help.

## Brand Personality

Warm, direct, human. Talks like a smart friend who happens to know tax law — not a lawyer, not a bank, not a government portal. Three words: **clear, close, capable**.

Emotional goal: replace dread with confidence. The user should feel like someone finally gets it.

## Anti-references

- **Gestoría tradicional** — stiff, bureaucratic, Hacienda-palette greens and institutional greys
- **Generic SaaS cream** — Inter + Lato + #7c3aed purple, Tailwind startup default, forgettable
- **Aggressive fintech** — dark mode, neon green, Revolut-style cold confidence
- **Cute / pastel** — excessive roundness, soft gradients, mascot energy, infantilizing

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity is the product** — if a section is hard to scan, it contradicts the brand promise
2. **Warmth through specificity** — concrete details (real tax form names, real user scenarios) build trust faster than generic reassurance
3. **Respect the user's intelligence** — no patronizing tooltips, no hand-holding copy; explain the hard thing once, well
4. **Calm confidence** — the visual tone should feel like a deep breath, not a pitch deck
5. **Show the outcome** — don't describe features; show what taxes look like after Klaro

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum across all pages
- Keyboard navigable, focus states visible
- Color contrast ≥ 4.5:1 for body text, ≥ 3:1 for large text and UI components
- Reduced motion: respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for scroll animations and transitions
